注解

Generalized form of transport equations:

(1)¶\[\frac{a\left(x\right)\cdot Y\left(x,t\right)-b\left(x\right)\cdot Y\left(x,t-1\right)}{\tau}+\ \frac{1}{c\left(x\right)}\frac{\partial}{\partial x}\Gamma\left(x,t\right)=f\left(x\right)-g\left(x\right)\cdot Y\left(x,t\right)\]
(2)¶\[\Gamma\left(x,t\right)\equiv-d\left(x\right)\cdot\frac{\partial Y\left(x,t\right)}{\partial\rho}+e\left(x\right)\cdot Y\left(x,t\right)\]

where \(Y\) is the function, \(t\) is time , \(x\) is the radial coordinate.

The boundary conditions are given by

(3)¶\[u\left(x_{bnd}\right)\cdot Y\left(x_{bnd},t\right)+v\left(x_{bnd}\right)\cdot\Gamma\left(x_{bnd},t\right)=w\left(x_{bnd}\right)\]

These equations were rewriten as a first-order ODE group

(4)¶\[\begin{split}\begin{cases} \frac{\partial Y\left(x,t\right)}{\partial\rho} & = -\frac{\Gamma\left(x,t\right)-e\left(x\right)\cdot Y\left(x,t\right)}{d\left(x\right)}\\ \frac{\partial\Gamma\left(x,t\right)}{\partial x} & = c\left(x\right)\left[f\left(x\right)-g\left(x\right)\cdot Y\left(x,t\right)-\ \frac{a\left(x\right)\cdot Y\left(x,t\right)-b\left(x\right)\cdot Y\left(x,t-1\right)}{\tau}\right] \end{cases}\end{split}\]
